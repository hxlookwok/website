Computer Engineering


What is computer engineering?

As a computer engineer, you...

  • Design, build and test hardware components and systems
  • Work with software developers to have hardware and software function together

Expertise in computer science and electrical engineering is combined into one field, offering computer engineers a unique job duty that sets it apart from computer scientists.

Computer engineering vs. Computer science

In computer science, you mainly focus on computer theory and software. In computer engineering, you mainly focus on computer design and hardware.
